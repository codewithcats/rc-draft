    Kitten

    Birth - 1 Year

    A kitten's growth takes place in two distinct phases. Their nutrition should be different during each growth phase. 

    Adult

    1 - 7 Years

    The adult cat's nutritional needs vary based on their age, lifestyle, special needs, and specific breed. 

    Mature Adult

    7+ Years

    A mature adult cat's energy needs change along with their activity level as they begin to age.

    Senior

    11+ Years

    The senior cat requires less energy than the adult cat, and needs a palatable, nutrient dense formula.

    X-Small™ & Mini™

    < 8 & 9 - 22 lb

    Very small and small dogs have unique energy requirements and long-life spans. Nutrition plays an important role in their overall health.
    Medium™

    23 - 55 lb

    Energetic in nature with a wide range of physical capabilities, the medium dog needs a balanced diet to maintain a healthy weight.
    Maxi™

    56 - 100 lb

    Adapted for hard-work, the large dog has unique physiological, endurance and energy support requirements.
    Giant™

    > 100 lb

    Bred for search and rescue work, the GIANT dog's body weight puts his joints under daily stress. His diet needs to support joint health.
